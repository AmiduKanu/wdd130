Our School

Welcome to AA School

The school is a private Co-educational, non-denominational school and will be open to the pupils between the of 11-18 years regardless of race, creed, colour or nationality. The school has made provision for a broad-based Curriculum and will be operated as a non-profit making TRUST and is located at the present site of the Above All School in 17 Rocklyn Street, New England.

Vision Statement

The vision at AA School is to prepare and motivate our students for a rapidly changing world by instilling in them critical thinking skills, a global perspective, and a respect for core values of honesty, loyalty, perseverance, and compassion. Students will have success for today and be prepared for tomorrow.

Site Name

Above All School

Purpose

We provide quality education for pupils in kindergarten, middle and high levels.

Audience

Our Nursery and Daycare program nourishes children from a multi-sensory point of view. Our curriculum develops thinking skills by incorporating music, nursery rhymes, poetry, colors, shapes, patterns, an assortment of learning toys, and story books.Children build on their social skills through imaginative play, games, and group activities. The program not only strengthens their intellectual abilities and friends, but also guides their coordination and physical abilities through fine and gross motor activities in our outdoor play area.Children who are 18 months or older may be accepted into our program. Please contact the school for more information.

Our curriculum has been recognized for excellence by educators and parents, and its success dates back to our roots. For three consecutive years, we have being named as the best performing school in BECE

Our High School Program is based on the Common Core Standards. This program prepares students to easily gain admission for University studies
